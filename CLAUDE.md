## What Out Mobility Is

Out Mobility is a **mobility infrastructure company**.

We build verified attention infrastructure — technology that connects brands to
audiences inside the mobility stack. We are not an ad agency. We are not a media
company. We are infrastructure.

**One-liner:** Out Mobility builds the software layer that turns vehicles into
verified, managed advertising and mobility infrastructure.

---

## Current Execution Focus

> **ONLY Out-door matters right now. Everything else is context.**

### Out-door
A verified in-car advertising network deployed through Android tablets in vehicles,
managed through a centralized advertiser dashboard.

**What it is:**
- Hardware-in-vehicle: Android tablets mounted in ride-hail, fleet, and taxi vehicles
- Managed content delivery: ads served via a cloud dashboard
- Verified impressions: trip data + device telemetry = verified audience delivery
- Operational SaaS: fleet operators and advertisers managed from one platform

**What it must feel like:**
- Infrastructure software (not an ad network)
- Operational SaaS (not a media buy)
- Mobility technology (not an agency)

## Operating Principles

1. **Capital efficiency first.** Every decision must justify its cost in execution speed or revenue.
2. **Infrastructure mindset.** We build systems, not campaigns.
3. **Out-door only.** No feature, doc, or decision should distract from MVP delivery.
4. **Investor-grade outputs.** Everything written here should be presentable to a serious investor.
5. **Simple language.** A layman must understand what we do and why it matters.
6. **Fast execution.** Bias toward action. Decide, ship, learn.
