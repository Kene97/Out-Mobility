# Vision

## One-Liner

Out Mobility builds the software layer that turns vehicles into verified,
managed advertising and mobility infrastructure.

---

## Mission

To make every vehicle a node in a verified, intelligent mobility network —
starting with the screens inside them.

---

## North Star

**Verified impressions delivered per month** across the Out-door network.

This metric captures hardware deployment, software reliability, and advertiser
value in a single number. Everything we do either grows this number or
protects its integrity.

---

## The Problem

Advertising inside vehicles exists today — but it is unverified, unmanaged,
and unscalable. Operators run screens manually. Advertisers can't prove
delivery. There is no infrastructure.

Out-door is the infrastructure layer that fixes this.

---

## The Opportunity

Vehicles are one of the last high-attention, captive environments that have
not been industrialized as advertising infrastructure. A passenger in a
ride-hail vehicle has no choice but to be in that vehicle. They are present,
stationary, and available for 10–30 minutes per trip.

We are not selling ads. We are selling verified, managed access to that
attention — and the infrastructure that makes it provable.

---

## 5-Year Vision

Out Mobility operates the dominant verified in-vehicle advertising and
mobility intelligence network across Africa and emerging markets.

The Out ecosystem — Out-door, Out-side, Out-leaf, Out-charge — forms an
integrated mobility stack where every product reinforces the others and
every vehicle interaction creates data, value, and revenue.

---

## What We Are NOT

- We are not an ad agency
- We are not a media company
- We are not a ride-hailing company (yet)
- We are not a hardware company
- We are not a content studio

We build infrastructure. We enable others to deliver value through it.

---

## Guiding Belief

The future of mobility is software-defined. The vehicles that move people
will also move data, value, and verified attention. Out Mobility owns that layer.
