# Positioning

## Category

**Verified In-Vehicle Advertising Infrastructure**

We define and own this category. We do not compete in "digital out-of-home"
or "in-app advertising." We are infrastructure for verified in-vehicle attention.

---

## Positioning Statement

For fleet operators and advertisers who need accountable, scalable in-vehicle
media, Out-door is the managed infrastructure platform that delivers verified
impressions through Android tablets in vehicles — unlike traditional DOOH or
manual screen setups, Out-door provides real-time campaign management,
trip-verified delivery data, and a centralized operator dashboard.

---

## Target Customers

### Primary: Advertisers
- Brand managers at consumer-facing companies (FMCG, fintech, telecom)
- Media agencies buying on behalf of brands
- Direct-response advertisers who need verifiable delivery

**Pain:** No reliable, verified channel to reach captive urban audiences at scale.

### Secondary: Fleet Operators
- Ride-hail fleets (aggregated and independent)
- Taxi and transport cooperatives
- Corporate and shared mobility operators

**Pain:** Vehicles generate no revenue beyond fares. Screens are unmanaged or missing.

---

## Differentiation

| Dimension | Out-door | Traditional DOOH | Manual In-Car Screens |
|---|---|---|---|
| Impression verification | Trip-level data | Estimated | None |
| Campaign management | Centralized dashboard | Agency-managed | Manual per vehicle |
| Operator revenue share | Automated | None | Ad-hoc |
| Scalability | Software-defined | Fixed infrastructure | Linear cost |
| Data | Real-time telemetry | Panel estimates | None |

---

## Competitive Moat

1. **Verified data layer** — impressions tied to trip telemetry, not estimates
2. **Operator lock-in** — once installed, switching cost is high
3. **Network effects** — more vehicles = more reach = more advertiser demand
4. **Infrastructure positioning** — not a campaign, not a vendor, a platform

---

## Brand Voice

**Tone:** Precise. Confident. Infrastructure-grade.

**Write like:** AWS announcing a new service. Not like an ad agency pitching.

**Use:** "managed," "verified," "deployed," "infrastructure," "network," "platform"

**Avoid:** "revolutionary," "disruptive," "exciting," "innovative," "game-changing"

---

## Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)

"Out-door is a verified in-car advertising network. We put managed Android
tablets inside vehicles — ride-hail, fleet, taxi — and connect them to a
centralized dashboard that lets advertisers buy verified impressions and
operators earn passive revenue. Think of it as the infrastructure layer that
turns every vehicle into a verified media node."

---

## Investor Framing

Out Mobility is building the AWS of in-vehicle media infrastructure.
The market is large, the infrastructure doesn't exist yet, and the data moat
compounds with every vehicle deployed.
