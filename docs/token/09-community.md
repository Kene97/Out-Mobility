# Community & Social

## Where We Communicate

Out Mobility communicates with the community in one primary place:

**X (formerly Twitter): [@OutMobility](https://x.com/OutMobility)**

This is the official source of truth for:
- $OUT token updates and announcements
- TGE date and launch details
- Ecosystem milestones and product news
- Partner and operator announcements
- Community events and initiatives

If you want to know what is happening with Out Mobility and $OUT,
follow @OutMobility.

---

## What We Post

We post signals, not noise.

You will not find:
- Constant price commentary
- Vague hype posts without substance
- Engagement bait with no real information

You will find:
- Real product milestones as they happen
- Network growth updates (vehicle counts, impression volumes)
- Token development progress
- Ecosystem news as each product advances
- Direct communication from the founder

When something meaningful happens, we post it clearly. When nothing
meaningful has happened, we do not pretend otherwise.

---

## Why X?

X is where the Web3 community gathers and where mobility and infrastructure
conversations happen. It is the fastest, most direct channel for the kind
of audience Out Mobility is building — investors, builders, advertisers,
operators, and early believers.

For now, @OutMobility is the primary channel. As the community grows,
additional channels may be established. They will be announced on X first.

## Community Values

Out Mobility is building a community that reflects the infrastructure it is
building: serious, useful, and real.

The community we want to attract includes:
- Advertisers and brands looking for new ways to reach audiences
- Fleet operators and drivers interested in the mobility economy
- Web3 builders and DePIN believers who understand physical infrastructure
- Investors who think long-term and value real utility over speculation
- Anyone who believes that mobility infrastructure should be open,
  verified, and community-aligned

If that sounds like you, you are already part of this.

## A Note on the Tone

Out Mobility is a company with serious ambitions. We are not a community
project looking for momentum. We are an infrastructure company that is
choosing to include a community in what we are building.

That distinction matters. It means:
- We will not hype things that are not real
- We will not make promises we cannot keep
- We will communicate setbacks as clearly as we communicate progress
- We will treat the community as participants, not as an audience

The token is real. The network is real. The work is ongoing.
Follow along and hold us to it.
