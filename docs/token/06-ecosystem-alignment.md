# Ecosystem Alignment

## One Token. Four Products. One Network.

Out Mobility is not a single product. It is a stack of connected mobility
infrastructure products, each one addressing a different layer of how people
and vehicles move through the world.

$OUT is the economic layer that connects them.

This section explains how the token aligns with each product — and why
a single token across all four products is more powerful than separate
economies operating in isolation.

---

## The Out Mobility Stack

```
Out-door    →   Verified in-car advertising
Out-side    →   Ride-hailing and mobility subscriptions
Out-leaf    →   Carbon credits and sustainability infrastructure
Out-charge  →   EV charging network
```

Each product is distinct. Each serves a different need. But they all share
the same physical network: vehicles, drivers, and the routes they travel.

$OUT is the token that makes that shared network economically coherent.

---

## Out-door — The Foundation

**Status: Active — MVP focus**

Out-door is where Out Mobility starts. It is a verified in-car advertising
network deployed through Android tablets mounted in ride-hail and fleet vehicles.

**How $OUT connects:**

- Advertisers pay for verified impressions using $OUT
- Drivers and operators earn $OUT rewards for uptime and verified trip delivery
- $OUT staking unlocks premium placement and advanced campaign features

Out-door is the first product to generate real $OUT demand. As the advertiser
network grows from dozens to thousands of active campaigns, the baseline
demand for $OUT scales directly with it.

**Why this matters for the token:**
Every advertising dollar that flows through Out-door creates a reason to hold
and use $OUT. This is real, recurring demand — not speculative demand.

---

## Out-side — Ride-Hailing and Subscriptions

**Status: Future**

Out-side is Out Mobility's ride-hailing product. It is built on the same
driver and vehicle network as Out-door, but extends into the mobility
subscription economy.

**How $OUT connects:**

- Riders earn $OUT cashback on completed trips
- Drivers earn $OUT bonuses for high ratings and consistent availability
- Subscription plans for frequent riders may be payable in $OUT
- Fleet operators who participate in both Out-door and Out-side earn
  combined rewards, creating strong incentives to stay in the network

**Why this matters for the token:**
Out-side turns every completed ride into an $OUT event. The more riders
and drivers on the platform, the more $OUT circulates through the network.

---

## Out-leaf — Carbon Credits and Sustainability

**Status: Future**

Out-leaf is Out Mobility's carbon and sustainability product. It converts
the verified trip data generated by the Out-door and Out-side networks into
carbon credit infrastructure.

Every EV trip is a measurable reduction in emissions. Out-leaf quantifies
that reduction, converts it into carbon credits, and enables those credits
to be traded or retired by organisations seeking to offset their footprint.

**How $OUT connects:**

- Carbon credits issued through Out-leaf are denominated in $OUT
- Organisations purchasing carbon offsets can do so using $OUT
- Drivers who contribute verifiable emission reductions earn $OUT as part
  of the carbon credit issuance process

**Why this matters for the token:**
Out-leaf adds a sustainability-driven demand layer to $OUT. Every carbon
credit issued and retired is an $OUT transaction. As ESG demand grows globally,
so does this demand vector.

---

## Out-charge — EV Charging Infrastructure

**Status: Future**

Out-charge is Out Mobility's EV charging network. As the vehicle fleet
transitions to electric, charging infrastructure becomes a critical layer
of the mobility stack.

Out-charge deploys EV chargers — initially across fleet operator locations
and ride-hail hubs — and integrates them into the Out Mobility network.

**How $OUT connects:**

- Charging sessions are settled partially or fully in $OUT
- Fleet operators who charge through Out-charge earn $OUT loyalty rewards
- Charger hosts (location owners) earn $OUT for hosting network infrastructure

**Why this matters for the token:**
Out-charge adds a high-frequency, recurring transaction type to the $OUT
economy. Charging is daily. Charging at scale means daily $OUT transactions
across the entire fleet.

## Future Data Products

The Out Mobility network generates a significant amount of verified mobility
data: route data, impression data, trip data, and audience data.

Future plans include a data marketplace where this data — anonymised,
aggregated, and permissioned — can be accessed by researchers, city planners,
and marketers using $OUT.

This is a future product. It will be developed after the core four products
have launched. It is included here because it is a meaningful part of the
long-term $OUT demand thesis.

## DePIN: The Bigger Framework

Out Mobility is a **DePIN** company — Decentralised Physical Infrastructure
Network.

DePIN is a category of Web3 that connects real-world physical infrastructure
(vehicles, chargers, sensors, screens) to decentralised economic models
(tokens, incentives, governance).

$OUT is the DePIN token for the Out Mobility network.

The vehicles are the nodes. The drivers are the operators. The advertisers,
riders, carbon buyers, and charging users are the demand side. $OUT is the
token that makes the economic model function at scale.

As DePIN grows as a category — and the verified utility of physical networks
becomes more understood — Out Mobility's position as a real, operating DePIN
network becomes more valuable. $OUT is the token that captures that value.
