# Tokenomics

> **Important:** All figures in this document are **proposed** and subject to
> change before the Token Generation Event (TGE). Final tokenomics will be
> published and locked prior to launch.

---

## Total Supply

**1,000,000,000 $OUT (1 billion tokens)**

This is a fixed supply. No additional tokens can be minted after the TGE.

The total supply was chosen to balance:
- Enough tokens to support meaningful distribution across all ecosystem products
- Enough scarcity to preserve long-term value as demand grows
- A round, legible number that is easy for the community to understand

## Allocation Breakdown

### Ecosystem Reserve & Treasury — 30% (300M)

The largest allocation is reserved for the long-term health and growth of
the Out Mobility ecosystem.

This pool funds:
- Protocol development and engineering
- Ecosystem grants for builders and partners
- Strategic partnerships across the mobility stack
- Emergency reserve and operational continuity

**Release:** Governed by the Out Mobility team and, over time, by community
governance. Released according to milestones and ecosystem needs — not on
a fixed time schedule.

---

### Community & Rewards — 25% (250M)

This is the pool that flows to the people who build and sustain the network:
drivers, fleet operators, early adopters, and active community members.

It funds:
- Driver and operator reward programmes
- Staking yield for long-term holders
- Early adopter allocation (waitlist community)
- Community campaigns and ecosystem incentives

**Release:** Distributed monthly via reward mechanisms as the network scales.
The pace of release is tied to network activity — more activity, more rewards flow.

---

### Core Team & Founders — 20% (200M)

The team building Out Mobility earns a meaningful stake in what they are
creating — but with a vesting schedule that ensures long-term alignment.

**Vesting:** 48-month vesting period with a 12-month cliff.

This means:
- No team tokens are unlocked in the first 12 months after TGE
- From month 13, tokens vest monthly over the remaining 36 months
- The team is economically committed to the long-term success of the network

---

### Strategic Partners & Advisors — 10% (100M)

Reserved for strategic investors, fleet operator partners, and advisors who
provide genuine value to the network — not just capital.

**Vesting:** 24-month vesting period with a 6-month cliff.

Partnerships that do not materialise will have their allocation returned to
the Ecosystem Reserve.

---

### Liquidity Provision — 10% (100M)

A healthy token needs healthy liquidity. This allocation is reserved specifically
for market-making and liquidity provision across decentralised and centralised
exchanges at and after launch.

This is not a team fund. It is not a development fund. Its sole purpose is to
ensure $OUT can be traded with minimal slippage from day one.

**Release:** Deployed at TGE and managed by the Out Mobility team and
liquidity partners.

---

### Public Distribution / Launch — 5% (50M)

A portion of supply is set aside for the initial public distribution — the
moment the community gets to participate directly.

This includes:
- Public sale or fair launch mechanism (structure TBD)
- Early community distribution
- Waitlist allocation for registered members

The form of public distribution will be announced prior to TGE with full
detail on eligibility, pricing, and process.

**Release:** At TGE, subject to the mechanics of the chosen distribution method.

## Distribution Logic

$OUT is designed to flow into circulation gradually, driven by real network
activity rather than arbitrary unlock schedules.

The core principle: **tokens should enter circulation because something real
happened — not just because a clock expired.**

Community and reward tokens unlock as drivers deliver impressions, operators
grow their fleets, and advertisers run campaigns. The more the network produces,
the more tokens flow — creating a direct link between economic output and
token supply.

---

## What Is Not In These Numbers

These tokenomics do not include:
- Price projections of any kind
- Return on investment expectations
- Guaranteed buyback or burn commitments
- Exchange listing valuations

Those are not ours to make. The numbers above represent allocation intent.
Value is determined by the market and by the network's real-world output.
