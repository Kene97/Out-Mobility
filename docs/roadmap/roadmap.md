# Roadmap — Out-door

## Overview

Four phases from zero to scaled infrastructure.
Each phase has a clear entry condition, exit condition, and success metric.

---

## Phase 0 — Foundation
**Status: Active**
**Duration: 4–6 weeks**

Build the operating foundation. No customers yet.

**Objectives:**
- Company setup (legal, banking, domain)
- Tablet hardware selection and procurement
- Dashboard MVP scoped and development started
- First operator conversations initiated
- Investor narrative defined

**Exit condition:** Hardware selected. Dashboard in development. 1 operator in pipeline.

---

## Phase 1 — Pilot
**Status: Upcoming**
**Duration: 6–10 weeks**

Deploy and prove the model in a controlled environment.

**Objectives:**
- 50–100 vehicles live with tablets installed
- Dashboard functional: campaign creation, scheduling, reporting
- First advertiser campaign running with verified delivery data
- Operator revenue share flowing
- First impression report delivered

**Exit condition:** Verified CPM established. Advertiser renews or expands. Operator satisfied.

**North star metric:** 100,000 verified impressions/month

---

## Phase 2 — Scale
**Status: Future**
**Duration: 3–6 months post-pilot**

Expand vehicle count and advertiser base. Shift from founder-led to process-led.

**Objectives:**
- 500–1,000 vehicles across 2–3 operators
- 10+ active advertisers
- Automated onboarding for operators
- Self-serve campaign tools for advertisers
- Revenue run rate visible

**Exit condition:** Predictable revenue. Repeatable sales motion. Clear unit economics.

**North star metric:** 1M verified impressions/month

---

## Phase 3 — Infrastructure
**Status: Future**
**Duration: 6–12 months post-scale**

Become the category-defining platform. Raise Series A.

**Objectives:**
- 5,000+ vehicles across multiple cities
- API-open platform: third-party advertisers and operators
- Data product: audience insights sold as a standalone offering
- First adjacent product exploration (Out-side or Out-leaf)
- Series A fundraise

**Exit condition:** Market leadership in launch city. Clear path to city #2.

**North star metric:** 10M verified impressions/month

---

## Non-Negotiable Constraints

- Out-door only until Phase 2 exit
- No feature scope creep: dashboard, tablet, verification — nothing else
- Hardware cost must be offset by revenue share within 12 months per vehicle
- Every phase must be investor-demonstrable before the next begins
