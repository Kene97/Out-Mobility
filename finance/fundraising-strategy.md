# Fundraising Strategy — Out Mobility

---

## Fundraising Philosophy

We raise to prove, not to operate.

Each round has a clear, binary proof point.
Money in → milestones achieved → next round raised from a position of strength.
We do not raise to survive — we raise to win.

## Valuation Logic

### Pre-Seed Valuation ($3–5M cap on SAFE)

Basis: Team + idea + TAM + market timing. No revenue yet.
Comparable: Other African mobility/ad-tech pre-seeds at $3–6M cap.

### Seed Valuation ($8–12M pre-money)

Basis: Revenue multiple on ARR + growth rate.
- $10K MRR × 12 = $120K ARR
- Software revenue multiple: 20–30x ARR (high-growth, emerging market)
- $120K × 25x = $3M revenue-based
- Add: market timing, infrastructure positioning, ecosystem optionality
- Justified: $10–12M pre-money

*Note: Early-stage African tech companies often command premium multiples to ARR
because ARR underrepresents the network being built.*

### Series A Valuation ($30–50M pre-money)

Basis:
- $1.2M ARR × 25–35x multiple = $30–42M revenue-based
- Plus: network value (5,000 devices = infrastructure moat)
- Plus: ecosystem optionality (Out-side, Out-leaf, Out-charge)
- Justified: $35–50M pre-money
