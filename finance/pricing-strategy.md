# Pricing Strategy — Out-door

---

## Pricing Philosophy

Three principles:
1. **Start below market** — remove friction from first advertiser decision
2. **Price on verified value** — not inventory (impressions verified, not estimated)
3. **Raise price with proof** — CPM increases as data moat strengthens

We are not selling cheap advertising. We are selling the only verifiable,
captive in-vehicle attention product in this market.
Price must reflect infrastructure, not media.

---

## Core Pricing Model: CPM (Cost Per Mille)

**CPM = cost per 1,000 verified impressions.**

All billing based on verified impressions delivered, not impressions scheduled.
This is the trust mechanism. Advertisers only pay for what we can prove.

```
Campaign cost = (Verified Impressions / 1,000) × CPM Rate
```

|---|---|---|---|

| Enterprise | Custom | $15,000+ | Large national campaigns, custom placements |

**MVP: Launch and Standard tiers only.**
Premium + Enterprise tiers unlocked when fleet reaches 500+ devices.

### Pricing Logic

**Why $6 CPM (Launch):**
- Nigerian digital display CPM: $1–4 (banner ads, low attention)
- Nigerian DOOH CPM: $4–8 (billboards, estimated)
- Out-door: captive audience, 20+ min dwell, verified — should command premium
- Start at $6 to be competitive with DOOH, prove the model, then move up

**Why impressions-based (not time-based):**
- Advertisers understand CPM — it's the industry standard
- Ties payment directly to delivery — no disputes
- Enables campaign optimization (pause low-performing, scale high-performing)

## Operator Revenue Share

Fleet operators receive a share of verified ad revenue per vehicle:

| Revenue Share | Rate | Stage |
|---|---|---|
| Standard | 35% of gross revenue | MVP default |
| Premium fleet | 40% of gross revenue | High-quality operators, low churn |
| Volume bonus | +5% above 100 vehicles | Incentivize fleet growth |

### Why 35%?

Market benchmarks:
- Digital display publishers typically receive 40–60% from ad networks
- DOOH screens in shopping malls: operators get 20–30%
- Transit advertising (buses, taxis): operators get 15–30%

35% is above transit DOOH norms, reflecting the value of exclusivity (we depend on their vehicles).
Lower than pure publisher norms, reflecting the platform services we provide.

**Operator monthly revenue per vehicle (at Scale stage):**
- 2,574 impressions × $7.50 CPM × 35% = **$6.76/vehicle/month**
- For a 50-vehicle fleet: **$338/month passive income**
- For a 200-vehicle fleet: **$1,352/month passive income**

This is meaningful revenue for fleet operators in Nigeria.
Median ride-hail vehicle earns $300–600/month from fares alone.
An extra $7–15/month per vehicle (at scale) is 2–5% revenue uplift with zero effort.

## Pricing Competitive Analysis

| Medium | CPM | Verification | Attention Quality | Targeting |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Out-door | $6–12 | Full (trip telemetry) | Captive, 20+ min | Geo, time |
| Traditional billboard | $4–8 | Estimated (panel survey) | Passing glance | Location only |
| DOOH screen (mall) | $5–10 | Estimated (footfall) | High (indoor, visible) | Venue only |
| Uber in-app ads | $8–15 | Device-verified | Passive (app usage) | Behavioral |
| Social media (mobile) | $3–8 | Click/impression | Distracted scrolling | Behavioral |

**Out-door advantage:** Only verified, captive, high-dwell medium at competitive CPM.
As we raise CPM over time, we're still cheaper than Uber in-app and comparable to premium DOOH.

| Growth | $9–13 | Network density + scarcity pricing |

## Discounting Policy

- Volume discounts: 10% for campaigns >$10,000 (keeps enterprise deals flowing)
- Annual commitment: 15% discount (quarterly pre-pay) — aligns operator and advertiser cycles
- First campaign: no discount — the $6 CPM IS the introductory rate

**No negotiating below $5 CPM for any tier.** Below that threshold, unit economics break down for operators and platform alike.

## Future Pricing Models (Phase 3+)

### Dynamic CPM
Real-time pricing based on:
- Time of day (peak hour premium)
- Current fill rate (low fill = lower floor, high demand = auction)
- Device location (premium routes at premium price)

### CPA (Cost per Action)
For direct-response advertisers who want measurable outcomes.
Requires QR code interaction or promo code redemption tracking.
Adds complexity — Phase 3 only.

### Subscription / Retainer
Monthly retainer for always-on brand presence.
Fixed monthly fee for guaranteed impression floor.
Simpler for repeat advertisers. Improves revenue predictability.
Explore at Scale stage when retain rate is >60%.
